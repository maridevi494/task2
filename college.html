WELCOME TO OXFORD ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Oxford Engineering College (ESTD-1998) has been started as a Technical Institution to impart highest quality and standards in the urban outskirts area in Tiruchirappalli in Tamil Nadu under aegis of eminent academicians, educationists and professionals. The sprawling campus of Institute has environment conducive for academic pursuits like class room teaching & research. It has spacious building with ample scope for future development. The Institute has close links with industries through Industry Institute Cell which gives platform for Faculty & students to undertake projects and research work .
